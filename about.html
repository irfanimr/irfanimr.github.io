About Me

Passionate developer with a love for creating innovative digital solutions

I'm a Year 2 student at Republic Polytechnic, currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Design & Development. My journey into tech started with a fascination for how design and code come together to build the digital experiences we use every day. Since then, I've developed a strong interest in front-end and full-stack development, and I'm excited to continue this journey by pursuing further studies in Computer Science.
